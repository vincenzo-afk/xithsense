# User Personas

## Persona 1 — Casual Ravi

**Age:** 24 | **Occupation:** Software engineer | **Fantasy experience:** 1 year

**Goals:** Quick team selection in under 5 minutes before deadline. Wants to win occasionally without deep analysis.

**Pain points:** Spends 20 minutes researching and still picks the wrong captain. Doesn't track toss impact.

**Feature needs:** One-click Safe team, captain explanation, basic insights.

**Quote:** *"Just tell me who to pick and why. I don't have time to read 10 articles."*

---

## Persona 2 — Grinder Priya

**Age:** 29 | **Occupation:** Data analyst | **Fantasy experience:** 4 years

**Goals:** Consistent profits from grand leagues. Plays 20–50 contests per IPL match.

**Pain points:** All her teams look the same. She knows she needs differentials but can't identify them confidently.

**Feature needs:** GL team mode, differential picks with reasoning, ownership estimates, multiple team combinations.

**Quote:** *"I need teams that can actually win a grand league, not just finish in the money."*

---

## Persona 3 — Expert Arjun

**Age:** 34 | **Occupation:** Fantasy tipster (10,000 Telegram subscribers)

**Goals:** Produce high-quality, explainable picks at scale for his audience. Needs credibility.

**Pain points:** Manual analysis takes 4+ hours per match. Hard to produce picks for 3 simultaneous matches.

**Feature needs:** API access, bulk team generation, per-player reasoning to share with followers, backtesting to validate his advice history.

**Quote:** *"I can't afford to be wrong. My reputation depends on accuracy and I need the data to back every pick."*
